About Me:

Music has been my passion since I picked up a guitar at at the age of 6. I'm mostly self-taught and continuously strive to improve my skills on guitar, bass and drums. I began studying Music Performance in 2013, where I was a founding member of the band Deadweight (later known as Shingen). We went on to play numerous shows locally, and in 2014 we released the EP 'Uprising', which was recorded, mixed and mastered by myself. Throughout my studies, I also booked and promoted live shows under the name 'Black Kite Promotions'. This ran for several years, hosting many shows of varying sizes featuring artists both local and international.

After Shingen disbanded in 2016, I wrote music and performed with numerous bands including progressive metal group 'Gaia's Stone', math rock trio 'Lou Thesz Press' and black metal band 'Ecorcheur'. From here, I went into higher education studying Live Audio Engineering & Music Production at Spirit Studios in 2017 before taking a sabbatical in 2019 to work full time. I intend to resume my studies in September 2021. I currently play guitar for Stockport-based punk band 'Mint Acid'.
